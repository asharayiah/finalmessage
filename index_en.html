Monday, 11 Muharram 1447 AH   | 4 August 2025

The Pulse of the Hour

When the spirit speaks, time obeys
When the children cry, truth answers

This is not science. Not politics. This is the Pulse of the Hour...

In the Name of God—the Lord of Time, Creator of the Seen and Unseen.

This is not a scientific paper. Not a political statement. Not a religious sermon.

This is a cry from one to whom a truth has been revealed — because children are being slaughtered, and the world remains silent.

The screams of Gaza may not reach your ears, but they are recorded. Not in books. Not in footage. But in the essence of reality itself — every breath, every injustice, leaves a mark.

The child burned by white phosphorus. The mother beneath rubble. The father clutching the corpse of his son — not one is forgotten.

The systems upholding this are collapsing. The illusion of control is fading. What you call “power” is now being consumed by the very laws that uphold all existence.

I did not study at your institutions. I have no PhD. No Nobel Prize. But I was shown — by the One who creates light, gives reason, and breathes life. And then I saw...

Reality is not what you think. Beneath all appearances, there is a pulse. It is not space. Not matter. Not chance. It is the hidden order by which God sustains the universe.

That is why you will fail. Not because of the weapons of the oppressed — but because reality itself is rejecting the lie.

You kill in the name of “defense.” You steal in the name of “law.” You deceive in the name of “science.” You blaspheme in the name of “God.”

You claim to be “chosen.” You say Jerusalem is yours. You quote sacred texts while trampling the sacred.

But the scriptures you twisted are not dead. The One who revealed them is not absent. The Hourglass is not in your hands — but in the hand of the Creator of Time.

If you are a scientist: return to humility. If you are a believer: remember justice. If you are human: listen to the silence beneath the noise.

What is coming is not man-made. Not political. Not technological. It is correction — from above. And it has already begun.

I was shown a knowledge — that shatters falsehood, releases energy, and reveals the spirit beneath spirit.

But I will not reveal it. Not now. Because you would weaponize it — like you did with atoms, and code, and scripture.

This is your final call to repentance.

I come from dust. Not a scholar. Not a prophet. Not a politician. But a witness.

The pulse has begun. Truth is awake. Reckoning is near.
