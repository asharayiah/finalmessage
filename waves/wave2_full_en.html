Wave 2 · Introducing Gravity into Quantum Mechanics

Publication date: September 01, 2025 (Asia/Amman)

GIQM: a minimal, testable scaffold (EFT + QFTCS + operational witness) without revealing proprietary mechanisms.

Framework Pillars

  1. GR as low‑energy EFT: Newton’s potential + tiny loop corrections; UV agnostic.
  2. QFT in curved spacetime: gravitational redshift/time‑dilation modify phase; universal decoherence for composite systems.
  3. Operational witness (BMV/QGEM): entanglement of two masses via gravity → mediator is quantum, no single‑graviton detection needed.
  4. Foil: Schrödinger–Newton as a contrastive, testable semiclassical baseline.

Minimal Equations (weak‑field, non‑relativistic)

g_00 ≈ −(1 + 2φ/c²),  H ≈ p²/(2m) + V(x) + m φ(x) + O(φ p²/(m c²), φ²).

EFT ties φ to graviton exchange; QFTCS supplies proper‑time redshift and decoherence terms.

Prototype Experiments

  • QGEM‑Lite (BMV‑style): levitated micro‑spheres; suppress EM/Casimir; measure entanglement witnesses.
  • Quantum‑clock interferometry: dephasing vs height and internal‑energy variance.
  • SN‑foil optomechanics: compare decoherence/localization scales to SN predictions.
